Nutrition Case Study

Rafael Ferreira

Age:21 years old
Occupation:Footballer (Inverness Caledonian Thistle FC)
Location:Scotland
Origin:Brazilian/Scottish

Background

Rafael is a talented 21-year-old footballer currently in the reserve team at Inverness Caledonian Thistle Football Club. Known for his "Brazilian flair," he was once a rising star, but recent injuries and declining fitness have left him on the bench. He is determined to use the off-season to return to the first team and regain his competitive edge.

Lifestyle & Training

Training Patterns:

  • Able to commit to 5-6 training sessions per week.
  • Experienced in the gym (5 years) and confident with weights.
  • Lacks tenacity in aerial duels and tackles, requiring increased muscle mass and strength.

Dietary Habits:

  • Admits to poor cooking skills and a reliance on convenience foods.
  • Meal timing around training sessions is currently sporadic and unplanned.
  • Diet consists of high-calorie, processed items including frequent takeaway pizzas.

Health & Wellness

Height:1.75 m
Current Weight:65 kg
Physical Activity:Professional Football
Stress Level:Low
Desired Weight:75 kg
Main Goal: Rafael wants to increase his muscle mass and body weight to 75 kg. He requires specific guidance on optimal meal timing around training and high-quality protein requirements to support hypertrophy. Beyond his current club, his ultimate aspiration is to play in the English Premier League.
